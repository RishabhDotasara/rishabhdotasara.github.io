Hey there! 👋

I'm Rishabh, a developer who loves building stuff and then writing about the chaos that ensues. This little corner of the internet is where I document my adventures in code, share the things I've learned (usually the hard way), and occasionally rant about why something that should work perfectly... doesn't.

## What I'm Up To

Most days you'll find me deep in the trenches of web development, wrestling with APIs that don't want to cooperate, databases that have their own opinions, and CSS that somehow breaks when you look at it wrong. I work with JavaScript, Python, and whatever else the project demands – because let's be honest, we don't always get to choose our battles.

## Why This Blog Exists

I started this blog because I got tired of Googling the same problems over and over again, only to find solutions scattered across 15 different Stack Overflow posts. So I figured, why not document my own journey? Maybe it'll help someone else avoid the same rabbit holes I've fallen into.

Plus, writing about code forces me to actually understand what I'm doing instead of just copying and pasting until it works (we've all been there, right?).

## When I'm Not Coding

You'll probably find me experimenting with new technologies, reading way too many technical blogs, or trying to convince myself that "just one more side project" is a good idea. I'm also that person who gets genuinely excited about a well-structured database schema.

## Let's Connect

Feel free to reach out if you want to chat about code, share war stories from the development trenches, or just say hi. I'm always up for a good conversation about tech, especially if it involves debugging horror stories or celebrating those rare moments when everything works on the first try.
